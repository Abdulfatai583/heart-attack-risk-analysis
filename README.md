## â“ Problem Statement
Heart attacks are often preventable, but healthcare systems struggle to **identify high-risk populations early** and **understand contributing factors**.  
This project converts patient health data into **clear, actionable insights**, enabling proactive intervention and data-driven decision-making.

---

## ğŸ“Š Key Insights
*Analysis based on **158,000 patient records**.*

- **High blood pressure** is the strongest predictor of heart attack risk.  
- **Moderate to high stress levels** significantly increase risk.  
- **Middle-aged adults** show the highest incidence.  
- **Urban populations** experience more cases than rural populations.  
- Low **physical activity and poor dietary habits** correlate with higher risk.
